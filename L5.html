LAP HELL - Pizza Pursuit

LAP HELL - Pizza Pursuit (formerly known as Lap Hell (Hotfix n' Ready)) is a lapping mod created by TheCyVap. This mod is the upgraded version of the Lap Hell Mod by redvoltron and adds a multitude of additions, along with making previously unlappable levels possible along with adding a new level called The Pizza Gauntlet. The main feature of Lap Hell - Pizza Pursuit is that the mod does not outright change the difficulty of Lap 3 unless necessary, but significantly increases the difficulty on Laps 4 and 5.

As noted on the Gamebanana Page (currently private), modifying LHPP for Difficulty or Gameplay purposes is not allowed by the Dev Team, Although, Reskins of the mod are allowed
