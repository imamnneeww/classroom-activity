The Philippines

The Philippines is an archipelago of over 7,000 islands located in Southeast Asia. It is known for its stunning beaches, diverse culture, and friendly people. The country is a popular tourist destination, offering a wide range of experiences from historical sites to vibrant cities.

In Dumaguete, you can savor delicious local dishes such as kinilaw, silog, halo-halo, buko salad, and suman. For accommodations, options range from budget-friendly guesthouses and hostels to comfortable mid-range hotels and luxurious beach resorts in nearby Dauin. Visitors can explore Rizal Boulevard for scenic views, visit Silliman University’s historic campus and museum, dive at the renowned Apo Island, enjoy the beauty of Casaroro Falls, and kayak at the picturesque Twin Lakes.
